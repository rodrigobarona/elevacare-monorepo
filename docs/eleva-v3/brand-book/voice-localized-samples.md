## Expert disclaimer (trust framing)

- **EN:** Experts on Eleva Care are independent professionals who self-certify their credentials and professional status. Eleva.care may request documentation and perform reasonable checks where required for platform integrity, regulatory compliance, or risk management.
- **PT:** Os especialistas na Eleva Care são profissionais independentes que auto-declaram as suas credenciais e estatuto profissional. A Eleva.care pode solicitar documentação e efetuar verificações razoáveis quando necessário para integridade da plataforma, conformidade regulamentar ou gestão de risco.

## How to use these

- Treat the **EN hero and subtitle** as the brand line; translate future campaigns in the same spirit (benefit + specificity + control).  
- Preserve the **“independent, licensed”** phrasing and the **expert vs platform** split in every language — it is a core trust line, not a marketing flourish.  
- When adding new locales, align formality to healthcare norms (e.g. `usted` in ES, `você`/formal in PT-BR) and keep conversational warmth.
