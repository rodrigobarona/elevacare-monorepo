# Eleva.care art direction

**Status:** Living  
**Visual tokens:** [`_context/clone-repo/eleva-care-app/src/app/globals.css`](../../../_context/clone-repo/eleva-care-app/src/app/globals.css)  
**Asset pack:** [`assets/`](./assets/)

Art direction for Eleva is **calm editorial + warm human health**, not “hospital stock” and not “tech startup neon.”

## North star

**Premium here means:** generous whitespace, readable type, true-to-life people, a **teal-first** color story with **one warm accent** at a time, and a clear organic motif (leaf/branch mark) used with restraint.

## Color behavior

- **Core:** Deep teal (`#006D77`) and sage (`#83C5BE`) anchor the brand.  
- **Warmth:** Coral and sand are **supporting**—use for human warmth, secondary CTAs, and soft section backgrounds, not for every CTA.  
- **Energy:** Red / purple / yellow highlights are for **rare** emphasis (e.g. large footer band, campaign moments)—never all three at full saturation in one small UI surface.

## Typography behavior

- **Lora (serif):** Emotional and narrative headlines—mission, long reads, “why we exist.”  
- **DM Sans:** Everything interactive and most body on web.  
- **IBM Plex Mono:** System-like detail only—metadata, labels, overlines, IDs.

## Layout

- **Marketing:** Section pattern = small mono overline, large serif title, sans body.  
- **Product:** Dense but clean—cards, clear hierarchy, no decorative clutter.  
- **Gradients:** At most one **strong** multi-stop gradient per page as a “hero” moment; keep mid-page sections calmer (teal + neutral).

## Photography

**Do**

- Real situations, natural light, diversity of age and life stage.  
- Show **agency** and comfort—women in everyday environments where believable.  
- Align imagery to service story (e.g. pregnancy, movement, support, cancer journey) without sensationalizing.

**Don’t**

- Cold clinical white rooms as the default look.  
- Staged “laughing at salad” health clichés.  
- Mixed lighting styles in one page without intent.

Reference files in the pack: [`assets/imagery/services/`](./assets/imagery/services/).

## Portraits (team, experts, advisors)

- **Current baseline:** Real headshots, neutral or soft backgrounds, professional but approachable.  
- **Aspirational standard (for future design cycles):** One consistent spec—lighting, crop, and either color or B/W across all leadership; update when a shoot allows.

## Illustration and iconography

- **UI:** Simple line icons (Lucide in product).  
- **Trust:** Small bespoke SVGs (e.g. expert verified) are **functional trust**, not decoration.  
- **Larger illustration:** No unified custom library in the MVP; commission only when a message cannot be told with photo + type.

## Motion

- **Subtle:** Hover lifts, gentle transitions.  
- **Respect** `prefers-reduced-motion` in web implementations.

## Social and campaigns

- Start from [`assets/social/eleva-care-share.svg`](./assets/social/eleva-care-share.svg) for composition: gradient + mark + wordmark.  
- Campaign art may add photography but should **preserve** legible logo and teal presence.
