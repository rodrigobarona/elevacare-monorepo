# Monthly + Annual Subscription Pricing Model

**Created:** 2025-11-06

**Status:** Proposed (Phase 2 Update)

---

## Executive Summary

Transitioning from annual-only to **monthly-first pricing with 20% annual discount** to:

- Lower barrier to entry (monthly vs upfront annual payment)
- Industry-standard pricing model (most SaaS uses monthly)
- Incentivize annual commitment with 20% savings
- Improve cash flow predictability with monthly recurring revenue (MRR)

---

## Industry Research & Standards

### SaaS Subscription Pricing Best Practices (2024)

**Monthly vs Annual Pricing:**

- **Standard Model**: Monthly pricing as default, annual with 15-20% discount
- **Average Annual Discount**: 16-20% (two months free equivalent)
- **Conversion Rate**: 20-30% of customers choose annual when offered
- **Cash Flow**: Monthly provides predictable MRR, annual provides upfront cash

**Typical Margins:**

- **SaaS Gross Margin**: 70-80% (industry standard)
- **Marketplace Commission**: 10-30% (depending on value-add)
- **Platform Fees**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (Stripe standard)

**Benefits of Monthly Pricing:**

1. **Lower Entry Barrier**: $49/month vs $490 upfront (90% less initial cost)
2. **Easier Trials**: Customers can test for one month
3. **Better for Seasonal Experts**: Only pay when active
4. **Psychological**: "Less than $2/day" vs "$490/year"

**Benefits of Annual Discount:**

1. **Predictable Revenue**: Lock in 12 months commitment
2. **Reduced Churn**: Annual customers churn 30-50% less
3. **Better LTV**: Lifetime value increases significantly
4. **Cash Flow Boost**: Upfront payment for operations

## Proposed Pricing Structure

### Community Expert

#### Monthly Plan

- **Price**: $49/month ($588/year if paid monthly)
- **Commission**: 12% per booking
- **Billing**: Charged monthly via Stripe
- **Cancellation**: Cancel anytime (effective next billing cycle)

#### Annual Plan (Save 20%)

- **Price**: $490/year (2 months free)
- **Commission**: 12% per booking
- **Billing**: One-time annual charge
- **Savings**: $98/year (16.7% actual discount)
- **Cancellation**: Full year commitment, refund pro-rated if canceled

**Break-even Analysis:**

- Monthly subscription cost: $588/year
- Annual subscription cost: $490/year
- **Savings**: $98/year with annual commitment
